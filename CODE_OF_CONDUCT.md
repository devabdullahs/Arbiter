# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone who participates — regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

In short: be kind, be constructive, and assume good faith. Help keep discussions focused on the project.
Behavior that makes others feel unwelcome or unsafe is not acceptable.

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its full code of conduct. Please read it for the complete standards and enforcement guidelines.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainer:

- Discord: **[@monster20](https://discord.com/users/170115708871507970)**
- GitHub: **[@devabdullahs](https://github.com/devabdullahs)**

Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed as promptly as possible.
